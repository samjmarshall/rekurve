## 💸 2. Payment Plan Design (per *$100M Money Models*)

Hormozi’s rule: *“Don’t cut the price. Spread the cost.”*
Payment plans expand your market without devaluing the offer.

| Step | Plan Type                                                      | Use Case                             |
| ---- | -------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| 1    | **Pay-in-full bonus** – e.g., save $1,000 or get bonus service | 30–40% PIF conversions               |
| 2    | **3-pay split** – short term, 3× monthly                       | For “Good” tier                      |
| 3    | **6-pay or 12-pay** – for larger offers                        | For “Better” and “Best”              |
| 4    | **Finance / Stripe Capital / PayLater**                        | Adds affordability w/o cutting price |
| 5    | **Trial-to-full** – apply small test fee toward project        | Used for pilot entry offers          |

[Referenced: *$100M Money Models* pp.101–104]

## 📊 3. CAC and Close Rate Framework

From *$100M Leads*, Hormozi uses a **3:1 LTGP:CAC ratio** — you can spend up to one-third of your **lifetime gross profit** to acquire a client.

| Tier   | Price   | Gross Margin (est. 80%) | LTGP    | Max CAC (⅓ of LTGP) |
| ------ | ------- | ----------------------- | ------- | ------------------- |
| Good   | $7,500  | $6,000                  | $6,000  | **$1,800**          |
| Better | $15,000 | $12,000                 | $12,000 | **$3,000–3,500**    |
| Best   | $30,000 | $24,000                 | $24,000 | **$6,000–7,000**    |

* **Expected close rates:**

  * 25–30% for *Good*
  * 15–20% for *Better*
  * 10–15% for *Best*
* **Channel efficiency:** LinkedIn outreach & warm intros keep CAC far below these targets in early stages.

[Reference: *$100M Leads* p.204; *$100M Money Models* pricing strategy sections]

## 🧩 4. Offer Anchoring & Value Communication

Hormozi’s hierarchy of value [*100M Offers p.172–175*]:

> Anchor high → contrast lower → remove risk → show ROI.

Example structure for sales call:

1. Lead with *Scale Package* (“full transformation, $30K, enterprise-level”).
2. Contrast *Growth Package* (“same 80% of outcome, half the cost”).
3. Close *Growth* or *Foundation* as the “smart choice.”

Add guarantees:

* **Performance Guarantee:** “50% efficiency improvement in 90 days or we keep working free.”
* **ROI Guarantee:** “5× return or refund the difference.”
  (*100M Offers*, *Guarantees Playbook* frameworks.)
