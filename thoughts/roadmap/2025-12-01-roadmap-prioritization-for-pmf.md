# Roadmap Prioritization for PMF

> **Date:** 2025-12-01
> **Status:** Approved
> **Goal:** Restructure GitHub project to focus 100% on pilot validation given 5-10 hours/week capacity

---

## Context

### Current Situation
- Pre-PMF, running free Release Pilot to validate use cases
- Website complete (M3)
- Locksmith friend identified as first pilot candidate - warm, motivated, pre-qualified
- 35 issues spread across 8 milestones, scheduled over 8+ weeks
- Current roadmap assumes 40+ hours/week capacity

### Constraints
- Working full-time; only 5-10 hours/week available for Rekurve
- Time off: Dec 20, 2025 - Jan 4, 2026
- Discovery conversations require coordinating with locksmith friend outside business hours

### The Problem
Current roadmap has validation scheduled 5+ weeks out (Week 6: Jan 5-11), with product/marketing work filling the gap. This is backwards for pre-PMF.

---

## Strategic Insight

From PMF research:

> "Pre-PMF, your single goal should be to make 10 customers very happy. Everything you prioritize should serve this goal."

> "Set one goal—one priority—so that you can align all of your initiatives."

At 5-10 hours/week, splitting focus across LinkedIn optimization, quick-win offers, and ROI calculators is counterproductive. These are **scaling activities** for after the model is validated.

---

## Decision

**Single priority: Get the locksmith pilot live and generating learnings.**

Everything else moves to Post-PMF backlog until real data validates the approach.
