# Value-Based Pricing Proposal Templates

## Overview

These templates shift pricing conversations from "What does this cost to build?" to "What is this worth to your business?"

**Expected Impact:**
- 30-50% higher average deal value
- Better client qualification (attracts results-focused buyers)
- Stronger retention (clients who pay for value stick longer)

---

## Understanding Value-Based Pricing

### The Problem with Cost-Plus Pricing

**Traditional approach:**
- Calculate time investment (80 hours)
- Add hourly rate ($150/hr)
- Total: $12,000

**The problem:** Your efficiency punishes you. If you get faster (60 hours), you earn less ($9,000) despite delivering the same value.

### The Value-Based Alternative

**What you should do:**
1. Identify client's current cost/loss (e.g., $475K annually)
2. Determine solution's impact (recover 50% = $237K)
3. Price as percentage of value created: 10-20%
4. Your price: $24K-47K for the same work

**Why it works:**
- Client sees 10× return regardless of your time investment
- You capture more value for outcomes delivered
- As you get more efficient, profit margins increase

## Value Discovery Framework

### Pre-Proposal Questions

**Goal:** Quantify their current state so you can price against measurable value creation.

#### Revenue Impact Questions

**Q:** "How many deals fall through the cracks each month due to slow follow-up?"
- **Follow-up:** "What's the average value of those deals?"
- **Calculate:** Lost deals/month × avg deal size × 12 = Annual opportunity cost

**Example:**
- 3 deals/month × $25K avg = $75K/month = **$900K annual opportunity cost**

---

**Q:** "What's your current lead-to-meeting conversion rate?"
- **Follow-up:** "If we increased that by 30%, how many additional meetings per month?"
- **Calculate:** Additional meetings × close rate × deal size = Revenue impact

**Example:**
- Current: 100 leads → 10 meetings (10% conversion)
- With AI agents: 100 leads → 13 meetings (30% improvement)
- 3 extra meetings × 30% close rate × $25K = **$270K additional annual revenue**

---

#### Cost Savings Questions

**Q:** "How many hours per week does your team spend on manual CRM updates and follow-up?"
- **Follow-up:** "How many people involved? What's fully-loaded hourly cost?"
- **Calculate:** Hours/week × 52 weeks × hourly cost × # people

**Example:**
- 8 hrs/week × 4 people × $50/hr × 52 weeks = **$83,200 annual cost**

## Pricing Models

### Model 1: Percentage of Value Created (Recommended)

**Best for:** Clients with clear, measurable outcomes

**Structure:**
> "Our fee is 10-15% of the first-year value we create, with a floor of $15,000 and a ceiling of $50,000."

**Example:**
```
Based on our discovery call:

CURRENT STATE:
• 3-5 deals falling through monthly = $900K annual opportunity cost
• 15 hours/week manual sales admin = $39K annual cost
• Lead response time: 48+ hours (should be <5 minutes)
• TOTAL QUANTIFIED IMPACT: $939,000 annually

CONSERVATIVE IMPACT ESTIMATE:
• Recover 30% of lost deals: $270,000
• Eliminate 80% of manual work: $31,200
• Total Year 1 Value: $301,200

OUR INVESTMENT:
• Value-based pricing: 15% of impact = $45,180
• Recommended: $35,000 (8.6× ROI)

GUARANTEE:
If we don't deliver at least $175,000 in measured value within 12 months
(5× your investment), we continue working at no cost until we do.
```

---

### Model 2: Performance-Based with Milestones

**Best for:** Clients who want predictability with value alignment

**Structure:**

```
OPTION A: FIXED FEE
• Project Fee: $25,000
• No performance clauses
• Standard deliverables

OPTION B: PERFORMANCE-BASED (RECOMMENDED)
• Base Fee: $20,000 (20% discount)
• Success Fee: $10,000 if we achieve:
  - 5× ROI within 6 months
  - 30%+ increase in qualified meetings
  - 80%+ reduction in manual sales admin
• Total if targets hit: $30,000
• Total if targets missed: $20,000

OPTION C: SHARED-RISK
• Base Fee: $15,000 (40% discount)
• Revenue Share: 10% of new revenue from system for 12 months
• Typical total: $25,000-$45,000
• We only win if you win
```

---

### Model 3: ROI Multiple

**Best for:** Clients who think in return multiples

**Structure:**
> "Our fee is priced to deliver 5× return in Year 1."

**Example:**

```
OUR FEE: $50,000

GUARANTEE:
If you don't achieve 5× ROI within 12 months, we either:
1. Refund the difference, OR
2. Continue working free until you hit 5×
```

## Objection Handling

### "That seems expensive compared to [competitor at $10K]"

> "Most agencies charge for their time. We charge for your value.
>
> **Competitor at $10K:** You pay regardless of results.
>
> **Our approach at $25K:** We guarantee 5× ROI. You're paying for $125K+ in measurable value, not hours.
>
> The real question: Which is lower risk?"

---

### "How do you measure ROI?"

> "We define measurement before starting:
>
> **Step 1:** Baseline your current metrics (meetings/month, conversion rate, admin hours)
>
> **Step 2:** Agree on value formulas (extra meeting × close rate × deal size)
>
> **Step 3:** Track monthly with shared dashboard
>
> **Step 4:** Quarterly review to validate numbers jointly
>
> It's objective, measurable, and transparent."

---

### "We don't have budget for $30K"

> "Let's explore options:
>
> **Phase it:** $12K Phase 1, then $18K Phase 2 after results
>
> **Performance-based:** $15K base + $15K only if 5× ROI achieved
>
> **Monthly:** $10K upfront + $2,500/mo, cancel anytime after Month 6
>
> **Reallocate:** Most clients pay $20K+ for tools we replace (CRM add-ons, sales intelligence, marketing automation). You're reallocating, not adding."

---

## Key Principles

1. **Always quantify value during discovery** - If you can't measure it, you can't charge for it
2. **Price below captured value** - Client should see 3-10× return minimum
3. **Use guarantees to de-risk** - Makes higher prices feel safer
4. **Offer multiple options** - Gives client control, higher acceptance rate
5. **Be confident** - If you waver on price, they'll negotiate

**Remember:** You're not selling hours. You're selling a $300K+ outcome for $30-50K. That's the bargain of the century for them.
