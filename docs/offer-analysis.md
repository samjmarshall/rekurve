## 💣 Grand Slam Offer Upgrade

**Core Promise:**

> “We help professional services firms recover 20+ hours per week and add $100K+ to their pipeline in 90 days — or we work free until you do.”

**Value Equation**

* **Dream Outcome:** More billable hours + bigger pipeline (+$100K+).
* **Perceived Likelihood:** Proof via case studies & quantified ROI.
* **Time Delay:** <90 days to result.
* **Effort/Sacrifice:** “Done-for-you” automation = minimal client lift.

### 🧩 Bonuses That Multiply Value Without Delivery Cost

1. **Automation Library Access:** Pre-built n8n templates ($0 marginal cost).
2. **ROI Calculator:** Quantifies savings (build once, reuse).
3. **Automation Roadmap Workshop:** 60-min group call monthly → leverage 1→many.
4. **Partner Perks:** Discounts on tools (Pipedrive, Clay, Zapier).
5. **Priority Access:** Slack channel direct with founder.
